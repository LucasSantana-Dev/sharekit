## When to use this register

Marketing register applies when: the brief calls for a marketing page, blog post, announcement, or thought-leadership surface. The goal is narrative engagement, credibility-building, and sometimes conversion. Register assumes a public-facing page where the audience is *reading* content, not navigating a product.

## What this register forbids

- No 4+ primary CTAs competing for equal visual weight in a single post.
- No metadata eyebrows (byline + date + "Read time" + category) on opinion pieces (news-article pattern, not thought-piece pattern).
- No prose paragraphs consistently <100 words (use 3–5 sentence rhythm).
- No marketing buzzword copy without concrete detail.
